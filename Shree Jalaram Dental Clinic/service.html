OUR SERVICES

Implant

Implants require an involuntary and expensive cosmetic dentistry procedure, but are a long-term choice for replacing missing teeth. They are an alternative to bridges using neighbouring teeth as anchors, as well as removable dentures lying on your gums. A surgeon implants them surgically into the jawbone.

Root-Canal-Treatment

A root canal is a procedure for fixing and preserving a tooth that is seriously damaged or contaminated. The treatment involves removing, washing and disinfecting the damaged region of the tooth (the pulp), and then filling and sealing it. A broken tooth, a deep cavity, prolonged dental treatment of the tooth or trauma are the common causes that damage the pulp.

Teeth Whitening

Over time, teeth, especially after smoking, taking certain drugs, or drinking foods and beverages such as coffee and tea, may become stained or discoloured. Your dentist will bleach your teeth using a chemical method in one of two ways. He may do an in-office process or provide you with a home-based device to use.

Diagnosis

Periodontal disorder is diagnosed through a periodontal examination by the dentist or dental hygienist. This form of test should always be part of your routine dental check-up. To measure the sulcus (pocket or space) between the tooth and the gums, a periodontal probe (small dental instrument) is used gently. Three millimetres or less is the diameter of a good sulcus and it does not bleed.

Crown & Bridge

Glass, porcelain fused with metal, resin, or ceramic materials may be made from crowns. Since crowns are expensive, dentists typically only recommend them when other procedures do not yield a pleasing outcome. Your bridge's success depends on its base. So, note that if you wear a bridge, oral hygiene is especially necessary in order to keep the remaining teeth safe.
